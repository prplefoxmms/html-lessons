Mathieu Stewart

Founder and Ceo of Ladlin Designs
Jeweller, Hand-Engraver, stone-cutter, identifier and setter

I am the founder of Ladlin Designs, a multi genre jewellery design business. I always had a keen interest in minerals and Gemstones when I was younger which later became the drive for me becoming a jeweller.

The idea came to me back when I was in College, I remember talking to a group of friends of mine at the time about jewellery design. What they said changed the view of how I saw jewellery afterwards. They said that they found a lack of diversity in jewellery style and taste. That in order for them to get what they wanted, it would have to be commissioned and that was an expensive option

That gave me a brilliant and profitable idea.
